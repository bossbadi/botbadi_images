BossBeats is a smarter music bot that has advanced features. It supports YouTube, SoundCloud, Twitch, stream URLs, file uploads, and more! Get started by typing $help.

Here are the main commands:

Command Description
joinJoin your voice channel
play [query]Play a song from keywords, url, or file upload
search [query]Search for a song or get the previous search results
leaveLeave the voice channel and clear the queue
skipSkip to the next song in queue
pausePause audio
resumeResume audio
stopStop audio
queue [query]Show the music queue/add song to queue
shuffleShuffle the queue
pop {index}Remove a song from the queue
clearClear the queue
volume [vol]Change/show volume (0 to 1000)
nowplayingShow the currently playing song, if any
seek {seconds}Skip to a specific time in the song
pingCheck my response time

Note the brackets {} and [] are only placeholders and should not be typed.
